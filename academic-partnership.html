Bridging Academia and Industry

Known for its focus on business and technology education, Walsh College in the United States offers a range of programs that equip students with the skills needed to succeed in today’s digital economy. Our partnership allows students to benefit from Walsh College’s expertise in delivering high-quality, industry-relevant education that combines theory with real-world application.

Partnership with: Qualifi-Elevate Education Standards

As an esteemed UK awarding organization, Qualifi provides a range of regulated qualifications recognized globally.
