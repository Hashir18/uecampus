NATIONAL SECURITY AGENCY CENTER OF ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE (NSA)

Walsh College is recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Cybersecurity Education by the NSA (National Security Agency Center of Academic Excellence). Walsh College aligns cybersecurity coursework with NSA defined standards.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (acbsp)

Walsh College is accredited Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) which is the leading specialized accreditation association for business education supporting, celebrating, and rewarding teaching excellence.

Higher learning commission (HLC)

Walsh College is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education, ensuring high academic standards.
