The BBA in Marketing at UeCampus equips students with essential skills for modern marketing, covering brand management, digital marketing, consumer behavior, and market research. Key outcomes include analyzing market trends, creating effective strategies, mastering digital tools, building strong brands, conducting data-driven research, crafting creative campaigns, and applying ethical principles. This program prepares students for success in the dynamic marketing industry.
