Overview

Embark on a transformative journey with UeCampus’s Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) programme in Business Leadership and Innovation. This programme is designed to equip experienced professionals with the knowledge, skills, and credentials needed to excel as visionary leaders in today’s dynamic business landscape. Through a blend of rigorous coursework, practical research, and real-world application, our DBA programme prepares you to drive innovation, lead change, and make a lasting impact on your organisation and industry. Awarded by Pole Paris Alternance (PPA), our DBA programme is recognised for its academic rigour and practical relevance.

Programme Overview:

Cutting-Edge Curriculum:  Our comprehensive curriculum covers advanced topics in business leadership, innovation management, strategic decision-making, and more.

Research Focus:  Undertake original research projects that contribute to the advancement of knowledge in your field and address pressing challenges faced by organizations.

Expert Faculty:  Learn from renowned faculty members who are experts in their respective fields and bring a wealth of industry experience to you.

Global Perspective:  Gain insights into global business trends, practices, and challenges.

Why Choose UeCampus DBA Programme:

Career Advancement: Position yourself as a thought leader and subject matter expert in your field, opening doors to top leadership roles and consulting opportunities.
Research Opportunities: Conduct in-depth research projects that address real-world business challenges and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in your field.
Personalised Support: Receive personalised academic advising, mentorship, and support throughout your DBA journey from our dedicated faculty and staff.
Prestigious Credential: Earn a prestigious DBA degree from Pole Paris Alternance (PPA) and gain recognition for your expertise and accomplishments in the field of business leadership and innovation.

Admission Requirements:

A master's degree from an accredited institution
Strong academic record and demonstrated research skills
English language proficiency (for non-native English speakers)

Qualification Earned Upon Graduation

Stage One – One of the following Diploma from Qualifi

Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) degree from Pôle Paris Alternance (PPA)
